About SQ Edutech

At SQ Edutech, we believe every student deserves the opportunity to pursue world-class education. With years of experience in international education consulting, we've helped thousands of students achieve their dreams of studying abroad.

Our mission is to provide comprehensive, personalized guidance that makes the complex process of studying abroad simple, transparent, and successful. From choosing the right university to visa approval, we're with you every step of the way.

Why Choose SQ Edutech? Expert counselors, proven track record, end-to-end support, transparent process, and partnerships with top universities worldwide.
